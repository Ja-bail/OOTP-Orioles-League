Phillies-Padres Deal: Barbosa for Peralta
Wednesday, January 20th , 2027
Trade winds have been blowing through Philadelphia. This time, a deal has materialized between the Phillies and Padres. According to sources inside the organization, Philadelphia will ship 24-year old minor league RHP Gabriel Barbosa to San Diego for 35-year old minor league LHP Wandy Peralta and $1,760,000 in cash.


In his career, Peralta has pitched 482 innings over 538 games, recording 403 strikeouts and a 3.83 ERA. His won-lost record is 29-27 with 13 saves.
