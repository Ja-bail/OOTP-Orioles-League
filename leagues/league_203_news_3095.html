The 2027 Major League Baseball Hall of Fame class is soon to be announced, and Alex Rodriguez is hoping it's his turn to get the call.

Rodriguez retired in 2016 at the age of 41 and would go into the Hall wearing the New York Yankees jersey.

His career numbers show Rodriguez is hitting .295 with a total of 696 home runs.
